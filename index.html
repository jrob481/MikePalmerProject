About Me

My Great Grandfather was a furniture maker. His son, my Papa, loved to make things out of wood. Some of his creations are still being used in my house today! His son, my Dad, was an electrical designer and always loved tinkering and building. Making things is in my blood. And I'm proud to say that both of my daughters also have a creative spark.

I like making things out of all kinds of materials using all sorts of tools. Since Christmas 2016, turning on the lathe has become my favorite way to create. From bowls, pots, boxes & pens, keychains & ornaments. Each piece is unique and done with ever growing skill.
